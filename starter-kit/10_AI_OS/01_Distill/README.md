# 01_Distill — Process Into Meaning

Distill is the **thinking station**. Here you take raw items from the Inbox and ask: *What does this actually mean? Is it worth keeping? What's the reusable takeaway?*

This is where capture becomes knowledge.

## What belongs here

- Inbox items currently being processed
- Summaries and key takeaways extracted from longer sources
- Your own commentary: "why this matters", "how I'd use this"
- Rough drafts of notes on their way to `02_Knowledge/`
- Open questions a source raised

## What does NOT belong here

- Permanent reference notes → promote them to [`02_Knowledge/`](../02_Knowledge/README.md)
- Unprocessed raw dumps → those stay in [`00_INBOX/`](../00_INBOX/README.md)
- Project status and decisions → those go to [`06_Projects/`](../06_Projects/README.md)

## How to distill

For each item, write a few lines answering:

1. **What is it?** One sentence.
2. **Why does it matter?** One sentence.
3. **What's the reusable takeaway?** The part worth keeping.
4. **Where does it go next?** Knowledge note, a project, or the trash.

## Outcome

A distilled item ends one of three ways:

- **Promoted** → becomes a durable note in `02_Knowledge/`.
- **Attached** → linked into a project in `06_Projects/`.
- **Dropped** → deleted, because not everything is worth keeping.

Distilling little and often keeps the whole OS lean and trustworthy.
