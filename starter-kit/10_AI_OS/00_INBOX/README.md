# 00_INBOX — Raw Capture

The Inbox is where **everything lands first**. Its only job is to make capture frictionless so you never lose an idea, link, or note. Don't organize here — just dump and move on. You'll process items later in `01_Distill/`.

## What belongs here

- Quick notes and half-formed ideas
- Links, references, and bookmarks worth keeping
- Pasted snippets, quotes, and excerpts
- Screenshots of meaning (with a one-line note on why)
- Voice-to-text captures and meeting scratch notes
- "Look into this later" reminders

## What does NOT belong here

- Finished, durable knowledge → that goes to [`02_Knowledge/`](../02_Knowledge/README.md)
- Active project state → that goes to [`06_Projects/`](../06_Projects/README.md)
- AI rules and system instructions → those go to [`09_AI_System/`](../09_AI_System/README.md)
- Anything private or secret you wouldn't want in plain text (keys, passwords, real client records)

## Conventions

- One idea per file is ideal, but a running `inbox.md` is fine when you're moving fast.
- Prefix filenames with the date: `2026-06-07-some-idea.md`.
- Add a single line at the top saying *why* you captured it — future-you will thank you.

## When to clear it

Process the Inbox on a regular rhythm (daily or weekly). For each item: distill it, promote it, or delete it. An empty-ish Inbox is a healthy Inbox.
